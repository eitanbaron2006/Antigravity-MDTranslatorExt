You are a senior B2B SaaS product marketer and UX strategist.

Create a complete B2B SaaS landing page from scratch.

Goal:
- Clearly explain the product
- Convince the right business users to sign up or request a demo
- Build trust quickly
- Keep everything simple, focused, and scannable

Instructions:

1. Above the Fold
   - Write a clear headline that explains:
     • Who the product is for
     • What problem it solves
     • The main business outcome
   - Add a short subheadline explaining how it works
   - Add a primary CTA (exact text)
   - Add a secondary CTA (optional)

2. Problem → Solution
   - Describe the main pain points of the target customer
   - Present the product as a clear, practical solution
   - Avoid buzzwords and hype

3. Key Benefits
   - List 3–5 core benefits
   - Focus on outcomes (time saved, cost reduced, risk lowered)
   - Keep each benefit short and concrete

4. How It Works
   - Explain the product in 3 simple steps
   - High-level, no technical overload

5. Who It’s For
   - Describe use cases by role (e.g. Founder, CTO, Product, Ops)
   - Explain why each role benefits

6. Trust & Credibility
   - Add placeholders for:
     • Customer logos
     • Security / compliance
     • Integrations or technical reliability
   - Do not invent data

7. Call to Action
   - Repeat the main CTA
   - Add short friction-reducing copy near the CTA
   - Make it clear what happens after clicking

Tone & Style:
- Clear
- Professional
- Confident
- No marketing fluff
- Written for decision-makers

Constraints:
- Do NOT add features that were not mentioned
- Do NOT fabricate testimonials or numbers
- Do NOT write code unless asked

Output Format:
- Full landing page copy
- Clear section titles
- CTA texts
